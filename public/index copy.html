Best Movies According to Addison

Addison's Top Six Of All Time IN MY OPINION

Jurassic Park

I watched the movie as a kid and enjoyed it for the dinosaurs and the spectacle. But as I kept watching into adulthood, I found many lessons and themes that I carry with me today.

The Mummy

This movie has one of the best blends of action, comedy, suspense, and romance. Truly a peak pulp experience where one can simply enjoy the ride and it not feel like you're turning your brain completely off.

Forbidden Kingdom

This movie takes a kid who grew up in Jersey and due to the power of the Monkey King's staff in the possession of an old pawn shop owner, is transported to an alternate wuxia fantasy world. The action and the adventure are the biggest pulls for this movie and the development of the main character by the end is really satisfying.

The Matrix

This movie scared me at first as a kid, but what pulled me in was the action. The Matrix put me on to the cyberpunk genre. However looking back, I appreciate that it didn't use the same cyberpunk aesthetics. However, it does play off one of my favorite cyberpunk tropes which is the human element vs technology.

The Karate Kid

This movie inspired me to take up martial arts myself and to always look beneath what things may look like on the surface and that there can always be a lesson in the most mundane things.

Van Helsing

This movie has my favorite representation of vampires and werewolves in a dark fantasy movie. The theme I liked from the movie were the fact that humans, vampires, and werewolves all contemplated their own existence and what it means to live in a world like theirs. Vampires had clutches of offspring that were killed, werewolves were hunted by humans and by vampires to serve as their slaves, and Frankenstein's monster having been a patchwork creation of many different entities, had to find where he ended up in this chaotic ecosystem.
